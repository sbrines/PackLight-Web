Everything you need to go lighter

Built by hikers, for hikers. No subscriptions, no cloud required.

Gear Inventory

Your complete gear library in one place. Track every item you own with weights, quantities, categories, and notes.

URL Auto-Import

Paste any product URL from REI, Zpacks, Gossamer Gear, ULA, and more — name and weight are fetched automatically.

Weight Calculator

Real-time base weight, pack weight, and skin-out weight. Automatic ultralight classification (SUL / UL / Lightweight).

Resupply Logistics

Plan every resupply box for long trails — mile markers, shipping addresses, pick-up status, and box contents with weight.

Smart Recommendations

Get gear recommendations based on your trip's elevation, season, terrain type, and duration. Never forget a critical item again.

Offline First

Everything stored locally on your device. Plan your trip in basecamp, on the plane, or deep in the backcountry — no signal needed.
