Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Overview

TrailWeight is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account or registration is required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reporting sent to third parties
  • No personal information of any kind

Data Stored on Your Device

TrailWeight stores the following data locally using Apple's SwiftData framework (iOS/macOS) or Room database (Android):

  • Gear inventory items (names, weights, categories, notes)
  • Trip details and pack lists
  • Resupply logistics
  • Weight history snapshots

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the app's built-in share sheet.

URL Auto-Import Feature

When you paste a product URL to auto-import weight data, the app makes a direct network request from your device to the retailer's website to fetch publicly available product information. No data from this request is sent to TrailWeight servers (there are none). The request goes directly from your device to the retailer.

Camera and Photo Library

TrailWeight may request access to your camera or photo library to add photos to gear items. Photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

Third-Party Services

TrailWeight does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

TrailWeight does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
